ABOUT HDS

Located in Silicon Valley, founded in 2000, we specialize in simultaneous 5 axis contour maching, 5 axis positional machining and precision turning. In addition, we also provide fast turn around prototype and production machining from various metals and plastics. Our dedicated, highly skilled team, together with our ability to innovate, improvise and manage set us apart from the crowd, bringing your most complex designs to life. HDS is your solution to time critical project requirements. We support your rapid prototype development by working directly from your DXF, IGES, and DWG files. When you need quick turn around you can't afford to stumble, Humpal Design Support consistently delivers quality, on time or sooner.
